How To Play

Basic Mechanics

Write to Fight

  • Every word you write in a day does 1 damage to the boss.
  • Writing under the daily average (1,667 words per day) damages the player and fills the boss's special bar.
  • Writing over the daily average may trigger the player's special abilities (depending on class).

Taking Damage & Getting KO'd

  • Every player starts out with either 50 or 75 hp (depending on class).
  • If a player's hp drops to 0 (by taking damage from the boss), the player is KO'd.
  • In the KO'd state, the player cannot do damage to the boss nor use special class abilities.
  • The player can revive from the KO'd state by writing 5,000 words; these may be written over the course of multiple days.

Boss Special Abilities

When the boss's special bar is filled, the boss will use a special ability. This may be an AoE attack (hits every player), a self-heal, or something else.

Classes

There are three classes, each with different abilities.

Fighter

The fighter is the main damage dealer.

Abilities:

  • Increase total damage for the day by 2.5% for every 250 words written over the base 1,667.

Healer

The healer class is the party's sustain.

Abilities:

  • Heal the other players in the party for 1.25 hp for every 250 words written over the base 1,667.
  • Revive a single KO'd player by writing 3,000 words in one day.
  • Revive all KO'd players by writing 5,000 words in one day.

Tank

The tank class protects the other two classes.

Abilities:

  • Has 75 hp instead of 50 hp.
  • Extra words are distributed among non-tank players who have written under 1,667 that day and counted as those players' own words.

    • This does protect other players from damage.
    • This does not prevent the bosses special bar from filling.
  • Can choose to take damage in place of another player.

Events & Bonus Damage

Words written at a region event (write-in, Twitter sprint, etc.) do 10% bonus damage to the boss.

Reporting Your Words

Report your word count and actions for the day in the Wordgrinder forum thread. Remember to count words written at a region event separately for the bonus damage!

For example, if I were playing a tank and wanted to take a hit for @seaweedbrained, my post might look something like this:

Words written on my own: 284
Words written at an event: 1526

I defend @seaweedbrained from the boss's attack, taking the damage in her place.

The cut-off for making your forum post for the day is 10:00 AM the next day (e.g. if you wrote words on Nov. 1, you must post about those words before 10:00 AM on Nov. 2). If you miss your post, it will be counted as if you wrote 0 words that day.

Taking Time Off

Even mighty writers and heroes need some time off. If you have a day coming up where you know you will not be able to write, post about it in the thread at least a day prior to your time off, and, while you will not damage the boss, you also will not take damage from your missed words that day.
